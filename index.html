Digital Wallet System

Merchant wallet data workflow — sales ingestion → billing → Stripe disbursement

Platform charges are applied per merchant billing settings before net disbursement is calculated.

Every money movement produces an immutable, append-only ledger entry with double-entry bookkeeping.

Key Design Principles

✦Immutable ledger — every entry is append-only; corrections use ADJUSTMENT counter-entries, never edits
✦Balance is derived — Pending, Available, and Reserved are always computed from ledger, never stored independently
✦Billing before disbursement — platform fees deducted from Available before net payout is sent to Stripe
✦Idempotent payouts — Stripe payout_id stored to prevent duplicate disbursements on retry
✦Webhook-driven finality — payout status (paid/failed) confirmed via Stripe webhooks, not polling
